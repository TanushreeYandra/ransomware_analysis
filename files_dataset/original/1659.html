His invention quickly gained popularity due to its convenience, safety, and ease of use compared to traditional gas-powered lamps. This innovation helped make electric lighting more accessible and affordable for homes and businesses worldwide. Over time, other inventors and companies built upon Edison's work, improving upon the design and introducing new technologies like fluorescent lights and LED bulbs.
